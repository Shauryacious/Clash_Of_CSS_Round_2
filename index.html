The Tesseract

The Tesseract, also known as the Cosmic Cube, is a powerful artifact in the Marvel Universe. It holds immense power and is sought after by many, including villains like Thanos and organizations like Hydra.

Iron Man's Arc Reactor

Iron Man's Arc Reactor is a fictional power source appearing in the Marvel Comics and the Marvel Cinematic Universe. It was created by Tony Stark as a clean energy source for his Iron Man suit.

The Arc Reactor also functions as the life-saving device that powers the electromagnet keeping shrapnel from reaching Stark's heart after he was injured. It has become an iconic symbol of the Iron Man character.

The Infinity Stone

When combined, the Infinity Stones grant their wielder omnipotence, allowing them to control all aspects of existence. Throughout the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU), the Infinity Stones play a central role, particularly in the events leading up to and during "Avengers: Infinity War" and "Avengers: Endgame".
